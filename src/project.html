Raising Litter Awareness

We use current, local data to inform the community of concerning environmental issues in hopes to enlighten the community on better litter habits

Below are our results for our data analysis for PGC litter Track data. This data is a collection of individual and group clean-up events throughout the county. We used this data to highlight areas of higher concentrations of illegal trash pick-ups through a heat map of the county. The bottom right is our heatmap analysis, where areas of higher concentration are represented in yellow. Most of trash that was reported was through individual and group clean-ups and were a result of illegal dumping of litter and trash.It is important we are aware of this issue and address it accordingly.

We offer an interactive map to the left of our results for residents of the community to locate areas for proper litter drop-off. Although this map is currently limited, in the future we hope to have a bigger vareity of places people can dump their trash with the option to filter based on the type of trash they have and the distance from their location.
